Maestro 2020

MAESTRO 2020 is a Business Challenge Competition, organised for the 3rd consecutive year by Rotaract Club of the University of Moratuwa. In cooperation with an external knowledge partner, we offer participants a worthwhile opportunity to address an issue faced by a real-world business entity. So MAESTRO 2020 is your chance to explore the entrepreneur in you , and be challenged by a panel of industry leading professionals.

Why Maestro?

  • To expose the youth to the real-life business environment in order to bridge the gap between their education and corporate requirements.

  • To create a platform for the Sri Lankan youth to showcase their entrepreneurial and leadership skills.

  • To identify promising young talent by delivering the chance to address real-life situations and identify solutions to these problems by applying their theoretical knowledge.

  • To give an opportunity to the industry to recognize the talents of their potential employees.
